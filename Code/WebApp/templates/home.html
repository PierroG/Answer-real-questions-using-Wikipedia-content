About us

Project

Askme is a Question Answering model built on the Stanford Question Answering Dataset (SQuAD) with a fine-tuned BERT. Askme is able to automatically answer factual questions without being aware of the context. He will find the information in Wikipedia and take the possible best answer. To try it, just type a question on the search bar.

Question Answering is a computer science discipline within the fields of information retrieval and natural language processing (NLP). We created this application as part of our Software project during our 2nd year of MSc Degree in Natural Language Processing.

Libraries : Pytorch, Transformers, NLTK, SpaCy, BERT, Wikipedia API, Flask framework.
